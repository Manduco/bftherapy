How to begin

Diagnosis provided by MD or PhD: ABA therapy requires an autism spectrum disorder diagnosis, which is provided by an MD or PhD. Diagnostic reports verifying ASD is required to begin.

  • Submit Service Request: Our goal is to make sure that you get the help you need for your child as quickly as possible after requesting services. We collect basic demographic information from caregivers and individuals receiving services and do our best to provide the assistance you require in a timely manner.
  • Authorization begins: Our clinical team will then evaluate your referral and obtain authorization for services based on your insurance coverage.
  • Therapy Begins: Care management is performed by a board-certified behavioral analyst (BCBA), who communicates with parents each week by phone or through in-person visits.

Therapy visits and activities will be determined by the treatment plan's goals. Our web-based electronic health record allows parents and caregivers to access data and notes at the end of each visit.
